Software Development Project (SDP)

Software Development Project (SDP) is a mandatory course in the VIT curriculum for all CSE and IT students in their final year. The aim of this project is to give students practical experience in developing software applications, working in teams, and managing software projects. Students work in groups of four or five to develop a software application of their choice. The projects are evaluated by a panel of experts from the industry and academia. SDP is a project course designed to give students experience in developing software systems. The goal of the course is to teach the fundamentals of software engineering in a hands-on manner.

Entrepreneurship Development and Innovation (EDI)

Entrepreneurship Development and Innovation (EDI) is a program offered by the VIT Business School to students who are interested in entrepreneurship. The aim of this program is to equip students with the skills and knowledge required to start and run their own businesses. Students learn about idea generation, opportunity recognition, business planning, marketing, finance, and legal aspects of starting and running a business. The program includes lectures, workshops, case studies, and guest lectures by successful entrepreneurs.EDI is a course that focuses on the development of large-scale enterprise applications.The course covers topics such as distributed computing, service-oriented architectures, and middleware technologies.

Data Analytics and Visualization (DT)

Data Analytics and Visualization (DT) is a program offered by the VIT School of Computing Science and Engineering to students who are interested in data science. The aim of this program is to equip students with the skills and knowledge required to analyze and visualize large datasets. Students learn about data preprocessing, data visualization, statistical analysis, machine learning, and big data technologies. The program includes lectures, hands-on exercises, and a final project where students apply the skills and knowledge they have learned to solve a real-world problem.

Course Projects

Course projects are a part of many courses in the VIT curriculum. They are designed to give students practical experience in applying the concepts they have learned in the course. Course projects can be individual or group projects depending on the course requirements. The projects are evaluated by the course instructor and are a part of the course assessment.Course projects are an integral part of VIT's curriculum. Students work on projects in various domains such as web development, mobile application development, data analytics, and machine learning. The course projects are designed to provide students with hands-on experience and enhance their problem-solving skills.
